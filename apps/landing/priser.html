FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about how modular pricing works

Can I change modules later? ⌄
Yes — you can add or remove modules at any time. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle. There's no lock-in and no rebuild required. Your data stays intact regardless of which modules you activate.
Is it really unlimited users? ⌄
Yes. All plans use team-flat pricing — you pay for the plan, not per seat. Bring your entire team, your contractors, your managers. No hidden per-user fees. This is intentional: we want your whole business to run on pixdrift, not just the people who can afford a license.
What's the Intelligence Layer? ⌄
The Intelligence Layer is a set of analytics and AI systems that sit on top of your operational data. It includes the Control Tower (real-time operational command view), the RCA Engine (root cause analysis for deviations and non-conformances), and PIX Analytics (trend detection, forecasting, and optimization suggestions). It's available as an add-on for any plan, or included in Enterprise.
What's the difference between a Module and an Industry Pack? ⌄
Modules are individual functional units (Workshop, Rental Engine, People OS, etc.). Industry Packs are curated bundles of modules + integrations pre-configured for a specific sector. An Automotive Pack, for example, includes Workshop + Rental Engine + OEM integrations + Cabas — all activated and connected from day one. Packs are always cheaper than buying the same modules individually.
Is there a free trial? ⌄
Yes — 14 days free on any plan, no credit card required. You'll get full access to the plan you choose (including any modules you want to test) and can invite your entire team. We'll help you set up and validate your stack before billing begins.
Can I switch from an old plan to the new modular pricing? ⌄
Yes. If you're on a legacy Starter or Growth plan, contact us and we'll migrate you to the equivalent modular plan. In most cases, you'll get more capabilities at the same or lower cost. Your data is never affected by plan changes.
Do you offer annual billing? ⌄
Yes — annual billing saves you approximately 20% compared to monthly. Contact us for annual pricing on any plan or custom bundle.
