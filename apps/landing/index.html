How it works

From lens to decision

quiXzoom and Optic Insights form a single pipeline: humans capture, AI structures, organizations act.

1
Field agents receive visual missions
Via the quiXzoom app, photographers and field agents get geo-tagged assignments anywhere in the world. One-tap accept, navigate, capture, submit.
2
AI processes and structures the data
Submitted visuals are analyzed in real time — object detection, change analysis, anomaly flagging — turning raw images into structured, queryable intelligence.
3
Optic Insights delivers actionable alerts
Enterprise customers receive event-driven notifications, dashboards, and API feeds. No raw footage. No noise. Just decisions: inspect, respond, plan.
4
Hypbit keeps the machine running
Internally, Hypbit tracks every deal, automates follow-ups, monitors product usage, and keeps the whole Wavult operation running — from sales to payouts.
