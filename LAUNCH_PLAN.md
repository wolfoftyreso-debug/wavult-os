## 1. ICP — Ideal Customer Profile

### Primary ICP: Growth-stage B2B Company

| Attribute | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| **Size** | 20–200 employees |
| **Stage** | Series A–C, or bootstrapped with €2M+ ARR |
| **Industry** | SaaS, tech-enabled services, professional services, logistics |
| **Geography** | Nordics (primary), DACH, UK (secondary) |
| **Pain point** | Scattered tools (Notion + Jira + HubSpot + Google Sheets) → coordination tax |
| **Budget signal** | Already paying €1K+/mo for tools they're stitching together |
| **Decision maker** | CEO, COO, VP Operations |

### Secondary ICP: Enterprise Team Unit

| Attribute | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| **Size** | Team of 20–80 within 500+ org |
| **Profile** | Ops lead or sales leader who's frustrated with corporate tooling |
| **Entry** | Bottom-up (team lead buys first, expands to org) |

### Anti-ICP (don't pursue)
- Solo founders / freelancers (too small)
- Heavily regulated industries with strict procurement (too slow)
- Companies with <€500K ARR (budget mismatch)

## 2. Pricing Strategy

### Positioning: Value-based, team-flat pricing

**Why team-flat (not per-seat)?**
- Removes friction to adding members ("just add them")
- Encourages expansion use
- Premium signal vs per-seat commodities

### Tiers

| Tier | Price | Sweet Spot |
|------|-------|-----------|
| **Starter** | €499/mo | 5–15 person ops team, first tool |
| **Growth** | €999/mo | Series A–B, dedicated sales + ops |
| **Enterprise** | Custom (€3K–€15K/mo) | Multi-team rollout, SSO, compliance |

### Early Access Offer (first 50 customers)
- 3 months free on any plan
- Locked-in pricing for 12 months
- Named in public "founding customer" list (social proof)
- Direct access to product team for feature requests

### Revenue target: 12 months
- **Month 3:** 10 paying customers → €10K MRR
- **Month 6:** 30 customers → €30K MRR
- **Month 12:** 80 customers → €80K MRR (€1M ARR milestone)
