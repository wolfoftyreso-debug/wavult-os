## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, QuiXzoom operates a crowdsourced visual intelligence platform (the **"Platform"**) that enables independent contractors ("Zoomers") to capture, upload, and deliver photographic and video content in connection with mission-based assignments;

**WHEREAS**, in connection with their participation on the Platform, Zoomers create and upload Content (as defined herein) that has significant commercial value to QuiXzoom and its group companies;

**WHEREAS**, the Parties wish to establish clear ownership and licensing terms with respect to all Content created and uploaded by the Zoomer via the Platform;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the access to the Platform, and the compensation payable to the Zoomer for completed missions, the Parties agree as follows:

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1** **"Content"** means all photographs, images, videos, audiovisual works, raw data files, metadata, geolocation data, timestamps, EXIF data, and any other digital materials captured, created, or uploaded by the Zoomer through or in connection with the QuiXzoom Platform, whether or not submitted in connection with a specific Mission.

**1.2** **"Mission"** means a specific task assignment posted on the Platform for which the Zoomer is engaged to capture Content at a designated location and/or time.

**1.3** **"Intellectual Property Rights"** means all patents, copyrights, moral rights (to the extent waivable), database rights, trade secrets, trademarks, rights in designs, and all other intellectual property rights of any nature, whether registered or unregistered, in any jurisdiction worldwide.

**1.4** **"Platform"** means the QuiXzoom mobile application, web application, API interfaces, and all related technology infrastructure operated by QuiXzoom.

**1.5** **"Derivative Works"** means any work derived from, incorporating, or based upon the Content, including but not limited to processed images, AI-trained models, datasets, composite works, and analytical outputs.

**1.6** **"Wavult Group"** means Wavult Group FZCO (Dubai), Wavult DevOps FZCO (Dubai), QuiXzoom Inc., QuiXzoom UAB, Landvex AB, Landvex Inc., and any other entity directly or indirectly controlled by Wavult Group FZCO.

## 2. ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

**2.1 Immediate and Automatic Assignment.** The Zoomer hereby irrevocably and unconditionally assigns, transfers, and conveys to QuiXzoom, with effect from the moment of upload to the Platform (or, in the case of Content created in connection with a Mission, from the moment of creation), all right, title, and interest in and to:

  (a) all Content, including all underlying works and materials;  
  (b) all Intellectual Property Rights subsisting in or relating to the Content;  
  (c) all Derivative Works created by QuiXzoom or any third party authorized by QuiXzoom; and  
  (d) all rights to sue for past, present, and future infringement of the assigned rights.

**2.2 Scope of Assignment.** The assignment in Clause 2.1 is:

  (a) worldwide and global in geographic scope;  
  (b) perpetual and irrevocable in duration;  
  (c) absolute and unconditional, not subject to any license-back except as expressly provided in Clause 5;  
  (d) inclusive of all renewals, extensions, and reversions of the assigned rights; and  
  (e) transferable by QuiXzoom to any third party without further consent of the Zoomer.

**2.3 Future Content.** This Agreement applies to all Content uploaded by the Zoomer during the entire period of their engagement with the Platform, including Content uploaded after the date of acceptance of these terms.

**2.4 Consideration.** The Zoomer acknowledges that access to the Platform and the mission-based compensation paid per completed Mission constitutes full and adequate consideration for the assignment herein. No additional payment shall be owed in connection with the exploitation of the Content by QuiXzoom.

## 3. MORAL RIGHTS WAIVER

**3.1** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law in each jurisdiction (including but not limited to the Swedish Act on Copyright in Literary and Artistic Works (*Lag (1960:729) om upphovsrätt till litterära och konstnärliga verk*), the Lithuanian Law on Copyright and Related Rights (*Lietuvos Respublikos autorių teisių ir gretutinių teisių įstatymas*), and applicable US state and federal law), the Zoomer hereby irrevocably waives all moral rights (including rights of attribution and integrity) in and to the Content.

**3.2** To the extent that moral rights cannot be waived under applicable law, the Zoomer agrees not to exercise such rights against QuiXzoom, its licensees, or any entity within the Wavult Group.

## 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

**4.1** The Zoomer represents and warrants that:

  (a) **Ownership:** The Zoomer is the sole and original author and creator of all Content uploaded, and has full right, power, and authority to enter into this Agreement and to assign the rights herein;

  (b) **No Third-Party Rights:** The Content does not infringe, misappropriate, or violate any Intellectual Property Rights, privacy rights, publicity rights, or other rights of any third party;

  (c) **No Encumbrances:** The Content is free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, licenses, and third-party claims, and has not been previously assigned, licensed exclusively, or otherwise encumbered in any manner that would conflict with this Agreement;

  (d) **Individuals Depicted:** Where the Content depicts identifiable individuals, the Zoomer has obtained all necessary consents, model releases, and permissions required under applicable law for QuiXzoom's commercial use of such Content;

  (e) **Property Rights:** Where the Content depicts privately owned property in a manner requiring permission, the Zoomer has obtained all necessary property releases;

  (f) **Applicable Law:** The Content was captured in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to data protection laws, privacy laws, drone and aviation regulations, and local photography regulations;

  (g) **Originality:** The Content is the Zoomer's own original work and has not been copied, in whole or in part, from any third-party source.

**4.2** The representations and warranties in Clause 4.1 are made at the time of each upload and are deemed to be repeated continuously throughout the Zoomer's engagement with the Platform.

## 5. PORTFOLIO LICENSE (RETAINED RIGHT)

**5.1 Limited Portfolio License.** Notwithstanding the assignment in Clause 2, QuiXzoom hereby grants the Zoomer a non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable, revocable, royalty-free license to display Content solely for the Zoomer's personal portfolio purposes (the **"Portfolio License"**).

**5.2 Restrictions.** The Portfolio License is subject to the following restrictions. The Zoomer shall not:

  (a) sell, license, sub-license, or otherwise commercially exploit any Content;  
  (b) use any Content for advertising or promotional purposes of the Zoomer's own business or any third party;  
  (c) use any Content in a manner that competes with QuiXzoom's business;  
  (d) remove or alter any copyright notice, watermark, or attribution associated with the Content; or  
  (e) publish Content in connection with any statement, endorsement, or implication that the Zoomer retains ownership of or commercial rights to the Content.

**5.3 Attribution.** When displaying Content in a portfolio, the Zoomer shall identify the Content as having been captured for QuiXzoom.

**5.4 Revocation.** QuiXzoom may revoke the Portfolio License at any time upon written notice if the Zoomer breaches any provision of this Agreement or the Contractor Agreement.
