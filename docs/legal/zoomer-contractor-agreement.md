## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, QuiXzoom operates a technology platform (the **"Platform"**) connecting independent contractors ("Zoomers") with mission-based visual data capture assignments;

**WHEREAS**, the Zoomer wishes to register on the Platform and undertake mission-based assignments as an independent contractor;

**WHEREAS**, the Parties wish to clearly establish the nature of their relationship and their respective rights and obligations;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein and access to the Platform, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1** **"Agreement"** means this Independent Contractor Agreement including all appendices and any Mission-Specific Order Forms.

**1.2** **"Platform"** means the QuiXzoom mobile application, web application, API, and all associated technology infrastructure.

**1.3** **"Mission"** means a specific, time-bound, location-specific visual data capture assignment posted by QuiXzoom or an end-client via the Platform.

**1.4** **"Mission Brief"** means the specifications, requirements, and acceptance criteria for a specific Mission, as communicated via the Platform.

**1.5** **"Deliverables"** means the photographs, videos, metadata, and other Content (as defined in the IP Assignment Agreement) produced by the Zoomer in connection with a Mission.

**1.6** **"Compensation"** means the per-Mission payment set out in the Mission Brief or as otherwise communicated via the Platform.

**1.7** **"Platform Rules"** means QuiXzoom's terms of service, community guidelines, technical standards, and any other policies published on the Platform, as updated from time to time.

---

## 2. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

**2.1 Nature of Relationship.** The Zoomer is engaged by QuiXzoom as an **independent contractor** and not as an employee, agent, partner, joint venturer, or representative of QuiXzoom. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create an employment relationship, a relationship of principal and agent, or any other form of employment-type relationship between the Parties.

**2.2 No Employment Rights.** As an independent contractor, the Zoomer:

  (a) is not entitled to any employment benefits, including but not limited to paid leave, sick pay, redundancy pay, pension contributions, health insurance, or any other statutory employment benefits;  
  (b) is not entitled to a minimum number of Missions or any guaranteed level of work or income;  
  (c) does not have a right of priority over other contractors on the Platform; and  
  (d) is not subject to QuiXzoom's internal employment policies.

**2.3 No Exclusivity.** The Zoomer is free to engage in other professional activities, including working for other platforms, companies, or clients during or outside the term of this Agreement, provided that such activities do not conflict with the Zoomer's obligations under this Agreement.

**2.4 No Substitution Right Restriction.** The Zoomer may, with prior written notice to QuiXzoom, arrange for a qualified substitute to perform a specific Mission on their behalf, provided that the substitute has registered on the Platform and accepted all applicable Platform terms including this Agreement and the IP Assignment Agreement.

---

## 3. MISSION-BASED ENGAGEMENT

**3.1 Offer and Acceptance.** Each Mission constitutes a separate, standalone engagement. The Zoomer is under no obligation to accept any Mission. Acceptance of a Mission by the Zoomer via the Platform constitutes a binding mini-contract for that specific Mission, subject to the terms of this Agreement and the Mission Brief.

**3.2 Mission Specifications.** Each Mission Brief shall specify:

  (a) location, date, and time window (if applicable);  
  (b) technical and content requirements;  
  (c) Compensation; and  
  (d) any special instructions or restrictions.

**3.3 Completion Standards.** The Zoomer shall complete accepted Missions in accordance with the Mission Brief and Platform Rules. QuiXzoom may reject Deliverables that do not meet the specified standards and may, at its discretion, withhold or reduce Compensation for non-conforming Deliverables.

**3.4 No Obligation to Offer Missions.** QuiXzoom is under no obligation to offer any minimum number of Missions to any Zoomer at any time.

---

## 4. CONTROL AND AUTONOMY

**4.1 Method of Performance.** The Zoomer retains full control over the manner and method by which they perform Missions, subject only to the technical and quality specifications set out in the Mission Brief.

**4.2 No Direction as to Hours.** QuiXzoom does not set, control, or direct the Zoomer's working hours except to the extent that a Mission Brief specifies a time window within which the Deliverable must be captured.

**4.3 Equipment and Tools.** The Zoomer shall provide and maintain their own equipment, tools, and materials necessary for the performance of Missions (including cameras, mobile devices, vehicles, and software). QuiXzoom shall not provide equipment or reimburse equipment costs unless expressly agreed in writing for a specific Mission.

**4.4 Location.** The Zoomer shall perform Missions at the location(s) specified in the Mission Brief. QuiXzoom has no control over the Zoomer's location or travel outside of Mission performance.

---

## 5. TAXES AND SOCIAL SECURITY

**5.1 Contractor's Responsibility.** The Zoomer is solely responsible for:

  (a) registering with relevant tax authorities as a self-employed person or business entity;  
  (b) filing all required tax returns and paying all income taxes, self-employment taxes, value-added tax (VAT), and any other taxes or levies arising from their activities as a Zoomer;  
  (c) paying all applicable social security contributions, pension contributions, health insurance contributions, and similar obligations; and  
  (d) maintaining any required business licences or registrations in their jurisdiction.

**5.2 No Withholding by QuiXzoom.** Unless required by mandatory applicable law, QuiXzoom shall not withhold income taxes or social security contributions from payments to the Zoomer. The Zoomer acknowledges responsibility for all such obligations.

**5.3 VAT / GST.** If the Zoomer is registered for VAT, they shall issue valid VAT invoices (where applicable under local law) for services rendered. If the Zoomer is not VAT-registered, the reverse charge mechanism may apply to cross-border transactions in accordance with applicable law.

**5.4 Indemnification for Tax Liabilities.** The Zoomer shall indemnify and hold QuiXzoom harmless from any taxes, penalties, interest, or other costs arising from QuiXzoom's failure to withhold taxes on amounts paid to the Zoomer, where such failure results from inaccurate information provided by the Zoomer.

## 6. JURISDICTIONAL PROVISIONS

**6.1 Sweden.** For Zoomers domiciled or resident in Sweden:

  (a) This Agreement is not subject to the Swedish Agency Act (*Lag (1991:351) om handelsagentur*), which is expressly excluded;  
  (b) The Zoomer shall register as a sole trader (*enskild firma*) or corporate entity with the Swedish Companies Registration Office (*Bolagsverket*) and the Swedish Tax Agency (*Skatteverket*) as required;  
  (c) The Zoomer is responsible for payment of Swedish income tax (*inkomstskatt*), self-employment social security contributions (*egenavgifter*), and VAT (Mervärdesskattelagen), as applicable;  
  (d) Swedish mandatory employment protection laws (*Lagen om anställningsskydd*, LAS) shall not apply to this Agreement;  
  (e) QuiXzoom shall issue an annual payment summary (*kontrolluppgift*) if required by Swedish tax law;  
  (f) Nothing in this Clause prevents the Swedish Tax Authority (*Skatteverket*) or a court from reclassifying this relationship as employment if the factual circumstances so warrant. The Parties intend and agree that the Zoomer is an independent contractor.

**6.2 Lithuania.** For Zoomers domiciled or resident in Lithuania:

  (a) The Zoomer shall register as a self-employed person (*individuali veikla*) or corporate entity with the Lithuanian State Social Insurance Fund Board (*Sodra*) and the State Tax Inspectorate (*VMI*);  
  (b) The Zoomer is responsible for payment of Lithuanian personal income tax (*gyventojų pajamų mokestis*) and social insurance contributions in accordance with Lithuanian law (*LR DK* — the Lithuanian Labour Code is not applicable to this Agreement);  
  (c) The Lithuanian Labour Code (*Darbo kodeksas*) shall not apply to this Agreement;  
  (d) VAT registration obligations under the Law on Value Added Tax shall be the Zoomer's responsibility.

**6.3 United States.** For Zoomers domiciled or resident in the United States:

  (a) The Zoomer shall be treated as an independent contractor for US federal and state tax purposes;  
  (b) QuiXzoom shall issue a Form 1099-NEC to any US-domiciled Zoomer whose annual Compensation exceeds USD 600, in accordance with IRS requirements;  
  (c) The Zoomer is solely responsible for self-employment taxes under the Self-Employment Contributions Act (SECA) (26 U.S.C. §§ 1401–1403);  
  (d) The Zoomer shall complete and provide Form W-9 (or W-8BEN for non-US persons) prior to receiving any Compensation;  
  (e) Misclassification provisions under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) and applicable state labor laws are acknowledged; the Parties intend that the multi-factor test applied by courts confirms independent contractor status based on the factual terms of this Agreement.

**6.4 Other Jurisdictions.** For Zoomers in jurisdictions not listed above, this Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, USA (Clause 14). The Zoomer is solely responsible for understanding and complying with the tax and labor laws of their jurisdiction.

## 7. COMPENSATION AND PAYMENT

**7.1 Per-Mission Compensation.** The Zoomer shall receive Compensation for each completed and accepted Mission as specified in the Mission Brief. Compensation is the sole payment owed to the Zoomer and constitutes full consideration for the assignment of IP rights as set out in the IP Assignment Agreement.

**7.2 Payment Method.** Compensation shall be paid via the payment methods available on the Platform (which may include bank transfer, payment processor, or other methods). Payments are subject to the Platform's payment terms.

**7.3 Disputed Payments.** Any disputes regarding payment must be raised via the Platform's dispute resolution process within fourteen (14) days of the payment date.

**7.4 No Expenses.** Unless expressly stated in a Mission Brief, the Zoomer shall not be entitled to reimbursement for any expenses incurred in the performance of Missions.

## 8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**8.1** All Intellectual Property rights in Deliverables shall be governed by the QuiXzoom IP Assignment Agreement, which is incorporated herein by reference and forms a part of this Agreement. The Zoomer confirms acceptance of the IP Assignment Agreement as a condition of engagement.

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY

**9.1** The Zoomer shall keep confidential all non-public information relating to QuiXzoom's business, clients, technology, Missions, and Platform operations that comes to the Zoomer's knowledge in connection with this Agreement.

**9.2** The confidentiality obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

---

## 10. PLATFORM CONDUCT AND COMPLIANCE

**10.1** The Zoomer shall at all times comply with the Platform Rules, all applicable laws, and all safety and legal requirements in the jurisdiction(s) in which they perform Missions.

**10.2** The Zoomer shall not engage in fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful conduct on the Platform, including the submission of falsified Deliverables, GPS spoofing, or impersonation.

**10.3** QuiXzoom reserves the right to suspend or remove any Zoomer's access to the Platform for breach of this Agreement or the Platform Rules.

---

## 11. TERMINATION

**11.1 Termination by Either Party.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing written notice (which may include in-Platform notification). Termination shall not affect any Mission in progress at the time of notice.

**11.2 Termination by QuiXzoom without Notice.** QuiXzoom reserves the right to terminate this Agreement and/or suspend the Zoomer's Platform access immediately and without prior notice in the event of:

  (a) breach of this Agreement or the Platform Rules;  
  (b) fraudulent conduct;  
  (c) conduct harmful to the reputation or interests of QuiXzoom;  
  (d) failure to maintain required registrations, licences, or qualifications; or  
  (e) any other reason at QuiXzoom's sole discretion, it being acknowledged that no employment-type protection applies.

**11.3 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination:

  (a) the Zoomer's access to the Platform shall be removed;  
  (b) outstanding Compensation for completed and accepted Missions shall be paid in accordance with normal payment terms;  
  (c) the IP Assignment Agreement, confidentiality obligations, and any surviving provisions of this Agreement shall remain in full force; and  
  (d) the Zoomer shall immediately cease using any QuiXzoom trademarks, branding, or materials.

**12.1 Limitation of QuiXzoom's Liability.** To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, QuiXzoom's aggregate liability to the Zoomer shall not exceed the total Compensation paid to the Zoomer in the three (3) months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

**12.2 Exclusion of Consequential Loss.** QuiXzoom shall not be liable for any indirect, consequential, special, or punitive damages arising from this Agreement.

**12.3 Zoomer Indemnification.** The Zoomer shall indemnify and hold harmless QuiXzoom and all Wavult Group entities from any claims, liabilities, damages, or expenses arising from the Zoomer's negligent, fraudulent, or unlawful acts, or from any breach of this Agreement.
